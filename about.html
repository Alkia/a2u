What is According2Us?

According2Us is free to download event participant counting application for smartphones.

Why According2Us?

Simply because the voice of each person voluntarily attending an event counts, it has to be properly counted. How many time have we seen a huge gap between the event organisators counting and the officials? One side may overstate and the other side try to minimize. With the Blockchain revolution, it is possible to have an indisputable unalterable counting.

 

Using According2Us

To use According2Us you need a compatible smartphone or tablet with an internet connection. The app generate a secured Universal ID, not back tracable, and your account is locked to the phone, although you can transfer your contacts over to new devices. According2Us is free to use.

 

Your security

Untracabity: from the data it is not possible to track back to your phone. On the other hand, if you want, you can prove you were counted

 

Features of According2Us

Users of According2Us can share their location in real time over messages. They can also organise lists of contacts so that they can quickly send messages to lots of people in group chats through According2Us. Probably the best feature of According2Us is that it allows users to keep in touch with people living abroad, without incurring the international charges associated with text messages.

 

Terms and Conditions and Privacy

According2Us terms and conditions specify that users should be at least 16 years of age to use the service in Europe. By default, According2Us will automatically set your Privacy Settings to allow any According2Us user to view your last seen, profile photo and status.
